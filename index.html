Overview

Linguamate is a platform that connects travelers with local students who can assist them with real-time language translation. Whether you're on a short trip or a long-term stay, our community helps you navigate language barriers easily and affordably.

Target Users

  • Travelers: Tourists, business professionals, and visitors needing language support.
  • Students: Local students fluent in local and foreign languages looking to earn or gain experience.

Core Features

User Profiles with availability and language preferences
AI or manual matching based on location and reviews
Secure payment & booking system
Virtual & In-person translator modes
Multi-language interface
Rating & review system for quality
